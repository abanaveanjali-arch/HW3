rose


A rose is either a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears. There are over three hundred species and tens of thousands of cultivars.


sunflower


The common sunflower is a species of large annual forb of the daisy family Asteraceae. The common sunflower is harvested for its edible oily seeds, which are often eaten as a snack food.


lotus


The lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) is a sacred aquatic plant symbolizing purity, beauty, and rebirth across many cultures, often rising from murky waters unstained. As India's national flower, it holds deep significance in Hinduism and Buddhism. It is a perennial plant with large, bowl-shaped flowers.
